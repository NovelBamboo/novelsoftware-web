- Custom software development
    
- SaaS platform development
    
- CRM implementation
    
- Marketing automation
    
- Enterprise knowledge management
    
- Generic AI chatbot deployments

# Complexity Rating

## Level

**Medium**

## Rationale

Novel Consulting is not building a software platform.

Instead, complexity comes from:

- Knowledge modeling
    
- Information architecture
    
- AI workflow design
    
- Governance
    
- Client-specific implementations
    

The underlying technology already exists.

The challenge is designing reliable systems around client knowledge.

---

# Assumptions

The business assumes:

- Clients already possess valuable intellectual property.
    
- Existing knowledge is worth organizing.
    
- AI performs significantly better when grounded in structured knowledge.
    
- Clients value reusable systems over one-off deliverables.
    
- Human review remains essential.
    
- Knowledge governance will become increasingly important as AI adoption grows.
    
- Small businesses prefer incremental implementation over large transformation projects.
    
- A successful pilot encourages wider organizational adoption.

# Risks

## Adoption Risk

Potential clients may not immediately understand the value of knowledge systems consulting.

**Mitigation**

Lead every engagement with a Knowledge Systems Audit and demonstrate one measurable workflow improvement.

---

## Technical Risk

AI tools evolve rapidly.

**Mitigation**

Keep the methodology platform-agnostic and focus on structured knowledge rather than specific technologies.

---

## Distribution Risk

The consulting category is relatively new.

**Mitigation**

Publish case studies, educational content, demonstrations, and thought leadership that explain the methodology through practical outcomes.

---

## Monetization Risk

Prospects may compare the service to lower-cost AI prompting or generic consulting.

**Mitigation**

Price according to business outcomes, implementation scope, governance complexity, and measurable time savings rather than hours or document counts.

---

## Operational Risk

Each implementation could become overly customized.

**Mitigation**

Standardize discovery, architecture, implementation, governance, and training while allowing client knowledge models to vary.

---

## Knowledge Governance Risk

Poorly maintained repositories lose value over time.

**Mitigation**

Offer Knowledge Operations as an ongoing advisory service that keeps repositories current, improves workflows, and prevents knowledge drift.

---

# Success Principles

Every implementation should satisfy the JOA standard.

## Justifiable

Every workflow exists because it solves a verified business problem.

## Observable

Knowledge, workflows, and deliverables are documented, measurable, and visible.

## Auditable

Every important output can be traced back to approved source material and documented decisions.

---

# Definition of Success

A successful engagement concludes when:

- Knowledge can be located quickly.
    
- AI retrieves approved information instead of generating unsupported content.
    
- Deliverables are assembled faster than previous workflows.
    
- Staff trust the knowledge system.
    
- The client can maintain and extend the system independently.
    
- Novel Consulting transitions from implementation partner to trusted strategic advisor.
