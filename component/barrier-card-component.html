Why Software Doesn't Get Built

Intimidation

Folks get lost in jargon, burned by off-the-shelf tools, and fear wasting money on solutions that don't fit their unique needs.

Technical Hurdles

Great ideas die because implementation feels out of reach. The gap between vision and execution stops progress before it starts.

Poor Communication

Agencies and developers often miss the vision. Translation gaps between technical and non-technical teams derail progress.
